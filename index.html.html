On record.

    Every machine has its own cadence. Technicians get reminders, supervisors see compliance — and SLAs are auto-logged.

    Vendor quotes, approval chain, spend category — all in one place. Approve on mobile, export for your auditor.

    Softseed AI reads your repair history and productivity data, and recommends the cheaper path to uptime. With a clear reason, every time.

Module 02 · Sales ops

Field teams that
actually show up.

Where are my reps? Did they visit? How many kilometres? Who's on leave? What about the new lead in Guindy? Softseed answers all of it — without anyone filling a single Excel sheet.

  • Live GPS & KM tracking
    See reps on the map in real time. Distance, route, time on site — captured by the app, not by memory.
  • Customer visits & check-ins
    One-tap check-in with location lock. Visit notes, order forms and photos attached — searchable forever.
  • Attendance, leave & permission flows
    Half-day, comp-off, casual, field duty — all supported. Managers approve or reject in one tap, audit trail kept.
  • Reports that run themselves
    Daily, weekly, monthly auto-reports by rep, beat, region or product. No more "send me the sheet by evening."

    Reps see only what they should. ASMs see their team. RSMs see their region. Finance sees numbers. No leaks, ever.
